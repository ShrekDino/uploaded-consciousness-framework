## Why Sponsor?

The CSDF is a formal diagnostic instrument for measuring where and how consciousness simulations fail. Every failure mode documented in the registry is a data point that constrains the hypothesis space for the next architectural iteration.

This research is conducted entirely in the open — no paywalls, no institutional gatekeeping, no proprietary data. Your sponsorship ensures it remains accessible to every researcher regardless of institutional affiliation or funding status.

- **Compute** — GPU/CPU time for running the simulation engine across hardware configurations

- **Research** — Formal analysis of observed failure modes, publication costs
- **Community** — Documentation, contributor support, review of pull requests
